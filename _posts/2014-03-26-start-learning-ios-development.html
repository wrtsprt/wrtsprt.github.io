Since I recently had the pleasure to start diving into iOS development, I wanted to jot down a handful of sources, that I found very effective.

  • There is the official Apple Introductory Tutorial - a good place to start, get an overview and your feet wet
  • Than there is an excellent, free(!) Stanford U Course on iOS development

These two should be good to get you started.

Ideally you also check out if there a CocoaHeads meeting near you.

For subject specific information I watched a couple of episodes of the NSScreencast.

Further reading objc.io and nshipster.com.

For German speaking audience: the UISprech Podcast.

Once you're past the basics, consider giving AppCode a shot. It is a challenge to the eyes, but oh man, is it efficient.
